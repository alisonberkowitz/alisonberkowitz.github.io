About Me

My name is Alison Berkowitz. I'm in the Class of 2016 at Olin College majoring in Engineering with a concentration in Computing. Every afternoon in the fall, I can be found coaching high school field hockey, hoping to pass on my love for the sport. On weekends I can be found at hackathons and CS conferences looking to meet new people and learn all I can. Both a nerd and jock at heart, I aspire to work in the intersection of fitness and technology.
